FAQs

What languages does VaakBhav support?
VaakBhav supports Hindi, English, and Hinglish (mixed Hindi-English) text. The model is specifically trained on code-mixed Indian language data.
How many texts can I analyze at once?
Up to 1,000,000 rows via file upload (CSV or TXT, max 200 MB). The text list and paragraph modes support up to 500 words per entry.
What do the sentiment labels mean?
Positive — the text expresses approval, happiness, or praise. Negative — the text expresses criticism, sadness, or disapproval. Neutral — the text is factual or balanced with no clear emotional lean.
Is my data stored?
No. VaakBhav processes text entirely in-session. Your data is never stored on any server and is discarded immediately after prediction.

Privacy Note

This tool does not collect, store, or share your data. All predictions happen on the server in real-time and are not retained. Session history is stored only in your browser's memory and is cleared when you close the tab.
